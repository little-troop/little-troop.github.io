Celebrating body hair as a choice, Project Body Hair made history, with Billie becoming the first razor brand to ever show hair in a razor ad.

With body hair as hero, the shoot was themed 'fuzz', forming direction for the campaign's styling, props, location, wardrobe and copywriting. From pom poms and fluffy socks to prickly plant life and brushed out perms, we made 'fuzz' a little more fun.

Y

The campaign launched with a 55-second video co-directed by Ashley Armitage with music by Princess Nokia. Within 24 hours the film reached over a million views and went viral.

The film has since had over 22 million views and over 2 billion earnt impressions in 23 countries.
