The ID represents the company’s vision for collaboration and takes cues from the magic of vintage film graphics, reimagined for new eyes.

The animated ident was seen for the first time at the 2022 Tribeca Film Festival premiere of TeaTime's debut full length feature Cha Cha Real Smooth.

We worked with Paris based animator Alexis Jamet to bring the logo to life with a nod to animated entertainment idents from the past.

We designed and developed a holding page for TeaTime as an interim while we work on the full site experience. The live site is embedded below.
