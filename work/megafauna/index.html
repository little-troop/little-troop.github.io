Identity, web design and build for artistic chef  XinYi Lim, and her ongoing venture, Megafauna.

XINYI-LIM

Little Troop was asked to create an offbeat, playful and custom identity for both Xin’s personal work and commissioned work, under Megafauna.

Each logo’s letterforms are built from pixel details, as a nod to the intricate layers and patterns found within Xin’s artful food projects.

Y

An ever-changing hero background updates on every site refresh, each created by LT from Xin’s own food imagery. Little Troop also brought in a playful filter navigation to instantly guide the user through Xin’s personal art projects, commissions and collaborations.
