We do

Founded in 2019 and led by award-winning Creative Directors Noemie Le Coz & Jeremy Elliot, Little Troop is a new creative studio that loves working with good people on smart projects.

While very diverse, our aesthetic approach often involves distinct sense of play, aiming to put the fun in functional. Strategically, our work aims to elevate brand offerings by distilling core messaging down to its clearest and freshest visual form.

Created from a mutual vision linked by the idea that anything is possible, Little Troop’s projects always aim to balance innovation with aspiration, emotion with product, and create an original, ownable point of view with true cultural relevance. We’re excited about building brands that we’d jump to choose ourselves, over the others — brands that we could trust ourselves, that exceed our own expectations and that we want around forever. To sum it all up, we want to make work that pushes past mediocrity, and instead, speaks in its own voice.

We love making new things.
